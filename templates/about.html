About the NDC Portal

The NDC Portal exists to alleviate the challenge of information asymmetry that currently plagues the international community in respect to UNFCCC PartiesNationally Determined Contributions (NDCs).

This app follows an API-first design, and is intended to provide interested parties with the information needed to analyze the similarities and differences between Nationally Determined Contributions. Using data compiled by theGerman Development Institute, each NDC is evaluated by 138 ranking qualifiers and metrics, which practitioners can utilize to gain insights, assess progress, gauge ambition levels, and craft policy.

For more information, please visit theNDC List for a general overview, view thefully browsable-API, and refer to theDocumentation Pagefor a detailed overview of the API!
